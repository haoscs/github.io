Short Bio


I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Center for Applied Internet Data Analysis (CAIDA), University of California San Diego, working with Dr. Amogh Dhamdhere and Dr. Alberto Dainotti.

I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science at the College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, VA, advised by Dr. Haining Wang, while was working with Dr. Haining Wang as a visting Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Delaware.

[2017/11] I defended my Ph.D. in College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, VA.

[2017/09] I am working as a Research Intern in Computer Security Department at NEC Laboratories America in Princeton, New Jersey.

Research


My general research interest lies in Networking and Security, with the emphasis on Internet-Wide Vulnerability and Security, Internet Measurement and Topology, Routing System and Security, Internet Infrastructure (Domain Name System, Content Delivery Networks, etc.), Network Attack and Defense, and Web Security and Privacy.
