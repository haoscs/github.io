Short Bio


I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science at the College of William and Mary, advised by Dr. Haining Wang. During my Ph.D., I worked as a visting research assistant with Dr. Haining Wang in Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Delaware.

I received my M.S. from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and B.S. from North China Electric Power University.


[2017/9] Currently I am working as a Research Intern in Computer Security Department at the NEC Laboratories America in Princeton, New Jersey.

Research


My general research interest lies in Networking and Security, focusing on Internet Measurement, Internet Topology, Internet Routing and Security, Internet Serving Infrastructure (Domain Name System, Content Delivery Networks, etc.), Network Attack and Defense, and Web Security and Privacy.
