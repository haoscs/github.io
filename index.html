Short Bio


I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Center for Applied Internet Data Analysis (CAIDA) at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), working under guidance of Dr. Amogh Dhamdere.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science at the College of William and Mary, while working with Dr. Haining Wang as a visting Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Delaware.

Prior to CAIDA, I worked as Research Intern in the Computer Security Department at NEC Laboratories America in Princeton, NJ.

Research


My general research interest lies in Networking and Security, focusing on Internet Measurement, Internet Topology, Internet Routing and Security, Internet Serving Infrastructure (Domain Name System, Content Delivery Networks, etc.), Network Attack and Defense, and Web Security and Privacy.
